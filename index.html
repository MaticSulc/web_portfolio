About me

My name is Matic Šulc and in was born on 6. 10. 2001. I went to primary school in Selnica ob Dravi and now I am a student of Secondary school of electrical engineering and computer science. Currently, I am in the 4th year. Besides all the schoolwork, I have been doing lightning and sound design for three years now. I specialize in lightning but more recently I have been learning more about sound as well. In all my school years, I have participated in a lot of extracurricular activities, my favorite being research papers. I think it has helped me a lot with my specialized subjects and my school performance in general. In my opinion it is great for us to have the opportunity to get to know our line of work and all the practical knowledge that comes with it so early. This year, for example, I made three research papers and I can tell from my own experience, that your own research that you must make gave me a lot of additional and very valuable knowledge, that I would never get otherwise. During the summer breaks of my high school years I have also worked some student jobs, just to make some extra money to spend during the year. I worked at Nova KBM as Junior Systems Administrator, where I took care of computers, networking and some general automation macros to keep my job easier. Looking at my technical side, I’ve been learning programming in my free time. I primarily work with backend web development, but I’m currently trying to merge with frontend web, since I know JavaScript quite well. I’ve also worked with C++ and Java for software development and done some web applications using the Node.js framework. Since some of my projects were IoT related, I also designed PCB’s for them. You can check out a few of my projects on the website and others on my GitHub page in the upper left corner.
